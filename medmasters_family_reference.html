02 Shared DNA

What makes them a family.

Four constants across all three brands. These are the identifiers that signal, at any level of recognition, that a piece belongs to the MedMasters family.

i

Parent naming

Every brand leads with "MedMasters" and follows with one distinguishing word. The single strongest family signal, free to deploy.

ii

Navy as anchor

Deep navy appears in every brand. Dominant in Collaborative, commanding in Launch Studio, quiet in Method. The through-line color.

iii

Rule of three

Each mark uses three primary elements. Three circles, three propeller blades, three concentric rings. Different shapes, same grammar.

iv

Modern sans

Every brand uses a modern sans family. Display faces flex per brand; body weight and structure do not.

05 When to Use Which

Pick a brand by audience, not topic.

The easiest way to know which brand a piece of work belongs to: who's buying. If a learner is buying a course, it's Collaborative. If a client is hiring you to build something, it's Launch Studio. If a peer is paying to learn your method, it's Method. The topic could be the same across all three; the audience is what routes it.

MedMasters Collaborative

Learning layered by design · Built for teams
  • Own-branded courses: Respiratory, Heart, Metabolic Edge, Critical Care
  • Clinical simulators and interactive tools
  • Board review content (NPLEX)
  • Kajabi-hosted catalog of learner-facing products
  • Student and learner communications

MedMasters Launch Studio

Built to ship
  • Client course builds for other educators
  • Business websites for clinical practices and small businesses
  • Custom AI tooling for practitioners
  • NFC cards, email sequences, Kajabi setups
  • Anything you're building for someone else

MedMasters Method

The practice of designing precision learning
  • Cohort course on your learning design method
  • Self-paced "Learning Architecture" program
  • Template and toolkit library for educators
  • Professional certification, when ready
  • Licensing of frameworks to other teaching faculty
